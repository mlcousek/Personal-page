# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow developers/endurance athletes who land on Jiří's personal site to get a read on who he is: a .NET developer at Monet+ and a marathon/ultra runner. They skim on desktop or phone, in any of three languages (en/cs/es).

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio and life-log: about/CV, blog (race reports, academic milestones), reading log, podcast log, video vault, and a sports page fed by live Strava data. Success = a visitor leaves with a clear, credible impression of discipline, technical depth, and athletic drive.

## Brand Personality

Disciplined, measured, high-energy. "Engineering precision meets endurance sport." Data is a first-class citizen (PBs, weekly km, charts) — numbers are part of the identity, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- The 2024-era AI aesthetic: cyan/violet/pink mesh gradients, gradient text headings, neon glows, glassmorphism on every card.
- Generic SaaS landing tropes (hero metric template, three equal feature cards).
- Anything playful/cartoonish; this is a serious personal brand with warmth carried by photography and copy.

## Design Principles

1. **Numbers are the brand.** Stats, times and distances render in mono, tabular, and animate with intent.
2. **One accent, locked.** Strava-orange carries energy; everything else is neutral ink on near-black.
3. **Borders over glow.** Hierarchy from hairlines, spacing and type weight, not drop-shadow halos.
4. **Motion is feedback, not garnish.** Enter/reveal under 600ms with strong ease-out; pressables respond; nothing loops without a reason.
5. **Photography does the emotional work.** Race and football photos are the color of the site.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for body text, full `prefers-reduced-motion` degradation, keyboard-reachable nav/modals, three-language i18n preserved.
